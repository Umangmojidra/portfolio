Grocery Sales &
HR Performance Analytics

Deep exploratory data analysis across 2000+ retail transactions and 6 months of employee performance data — uncovering sales trends, top products, staff productivity patterns, and strategic business insights.

Python · PandasMatplotlib · Seaborn EDABusiness Analytics

Project Overview

A multi-dimensional business analytics project combining retail sales data, employee performance metrics, and HR analysis into a unified reporting system — giving management a 360° view of business performance.

Built a Flask dashboard app alongside Jupyter notebooks to interactively explore the data and present findings to stakeholders.

Datasets Analysed

  • 2000+ retail transactions — product sales, category performance, revenue trends
  • Employee master data — roles, departments, tenure, compensation
  • 6-month performance data — targets, achievement rates, productivity scores

Sales Analysis Findings

  • Top 10 revenue-generating products and their seasonal patterns
  • Category-level performance — which product lines drive profitability
  • Peak sales hours and days for inventory planning
  • Monthly revenue trends with growth rate calculations
  • Customer basket size analysis and upsell opportunities

HR & Employee Performance

  • Department-level productivity scoring and ranking
  • Target vs achievement analysis — identifying high performers and gaps
  • Tenure vs performance correlation — retention impact on productivity
  • Salary distribution and department cost analysis
  • Absenteeism patterns linked to performance drops

Business Impact

The combined analysis delivered 12+ actionable business recommendations — from inventory reallocation and staffing optimisation to incentive structure redesign. Management now has a data-backed basis for every major operational decision.
